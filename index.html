About Us

What is
Gorengan?

"Gorengan" is a term used in Indonesian cuisine to refer to various types of deep-fried snacks, typically made from ingredients such as flour, vegetables, meat, or seafood. Gorengan can be found in many places in Indonesia, from street food vendors to traditional markets and restaurants.

Some popular types of gorengan include pisang goreng (fried banana), tahu goreng (fried tofu), tempe goreng (fried tempeh), bakwan (vegetable fritters), and martabak (pancake-like dish with various fillings). Gorengan is often served as a snack or appetizer, and it is a favorite among Indonesians due to its crispy and savory taste.
